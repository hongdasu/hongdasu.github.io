Design of a Thermal Metastructure for Thermal Camouflage

Jun. 2025 - Present

with Prof. Liqun He (School of Engineering Science, USTC, China)

Proposed and validated a method of designing thermal metamaterials to reshape heat sources during transient heat conduction

  • Performed thermal field transformation using Coordinate Transformation Theory
  • Developed 3D models and conducted the FEA simulations with COMSOL Multiphysics and Matlab
  • Experimentally verified the design's feasibility and efficiency with metallic 3D-printed prototypes.

Jul. 2024 - Dec. 2024
